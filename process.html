Snowmaking Introduction

The video on the right side is from a meeting held by the Central Cross Country Ski Association where a representative from HKD Snowmakers had a presentation on their snowmaking methods. This is not the same group the Edina Nordic Ski team chose, however the video does provide good information about the differences between "real" snow and manmade snow. Additionally, this is just another option for snowmaking equipment.





Edina's Method

Edina's snowmaking set up includes: Access to the city's water supply, a rented air compressor from Ziegler, the Cornice III Snowmaker from CHS Snowmakers, a high-pressure Davey water pump, multiple snow hoses, and a rented Sno-Cat to help move snow around the trails.
